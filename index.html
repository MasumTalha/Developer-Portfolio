#MS-903 "Doraemon" or simply Doraemon is the main, titular character of all Doraemon media (including, but not limited to the original manga, anime, video games, and movies). He is a robot cat from the 22nd century who travels back to 20th century to help improve the miserable life of Nobita, who will become the great-great-grandfather of Sewashi, Doraemon's friend. Doraemon's is usually represented by the color blue.
